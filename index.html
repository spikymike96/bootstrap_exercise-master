What is 'Gig For Days'?

Looking to play shows in cities you've never been before?

Gig For Days is a brand new site designed to help bring musicians and promoters together!
Are you a musician looking to play shows in new cities but don't have the connections?
Are you a promoter looking to put on new and emerging acts?
Gig Finder is for you!

How does it work?

As a musician you're given the ability to discover events in a vast number of different areas across the country. Discover the new breed of bands who are playing throughout the UK, AND if there's still space on a bill, you can apply to also play the show!

As a promoter you're able to showcase events that you're hosting to an even larger audience than ever before. Find and select new and exciting acts to play your show and craft the perfect lineup!
